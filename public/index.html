ABOUT SERIES

The story of Harry Potter follows a young boy named Harry who discovers that he is a wizard. He was raised by his cruel aunt and uncle, the Dursleys, who kept him in the dark about his true heritage. On his 11th birthday, Harry learns that he is a wizard and is invited to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

At Hogwarts,Harry makes new friends, like Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, and learns about magic, spells, and potions. He also learns that his parents, who were wizards, were killed by a dark wizard named Lord Voldemort when Harry was just a baby. Harry survived Voldemort's attack and has a lightning-shaped scar on his forehead as a reminder. As Harry grows older, he discovers that Voldemort is still alive and trying to return to power.

Throughout the years at Hogwarts, Harry faces many challenges, such as dark creatures, dangerous spells, and evil forces. He also learns about his connection to Voldemort and the importance of stopping him once and for all. With the help of his friends and allies, Harry confronts Voldemort and his followers, known as Death Eaters, and uncovers secrets about his family and past.

In the end, Harry must find and destroy powerful magical objects called Horcruxes, which are pieces of Voldemort's soul, in order to defeat him. The story is about friendship, bravery, love, and the battle between good and evil.

Through his journey, Harry learns important lessons about loyalty, sacrifice, and the power of love. The series has seven books, each following Harry's adventures as he grows from a young, inexperienced wizard into a hero who faces the ultimate challenge: stopping Voldemort and saving the wizarding world.

CHARACTERS

The "Harry Potter" series features iconic characters such as Harry, the brave boy who survived Voldemort's curse; Hermoine , a brilliant witch and loyal friend; Ron, a kind-hearted and courageous companion; Dumbledore , the wise headmaster who guides them; and Voldemort, the dark wizard who threatens the wizarding world. These characters, along with many others, form the heart of an epic battle between good and evil.
